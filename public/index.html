Frequent questions

How can I book?

We suggest you to make your reservation through our official website. You can also book at your trusted travel agency.

Are pets allowed?

Yes, they are. No extra charges are applied.

Is there wifi in all hotels?

Yes, in all our hotels you can enjoy free wifi.

Payment methods

We accept: VISA, MasterCard, American Express, Paypal and Binance.

Cancellation policy

For stays of less than a week it is important to notify 3 days in advance, otherwise you will be penalized.

How can I modify my reservation?

To modify a reservation that you have made on our website, consult the "My Reservation" page here or contact our agents by calling 000 111 222. If the reservation has been made through a travel agency or another web page, you should contact them to request any modification through them.
